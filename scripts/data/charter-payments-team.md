# Payments Team Charter

## Mission

The Payments team owns the end-to-end payment processing experience. Our mission
is to provide a reliable, secure, and developer-friendly payment platform that
supports multiple payment providers and handles thousands of transactions per
second with zero data loss.

## Responsibilities

The team is responsible for the payment gateway service, the settlement
reconciliation system, the fraud detection pipeline, and the provider adapter
framework. We also maintain the payment-related database schemas, event
definitions, and API contracts. Performance SLOs, incident response for payment
issues, and provider certification are team responsibilities.

## SLA Commitments

Payment intent creation must complete within 200ms at p95. Refunds must be
initiated within 1 second at p99. The payment processing pipeline must maintain
99.99% availability during business hours (06:00-22:00 UTC). Settlement reports
must be generated by 04:00 UTC daily for the previous business day.
